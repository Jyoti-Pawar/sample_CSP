Data Science

Data science is an extensive field that refers to the collection, concepts, tools and technology that enables the evaluation, investigation and extraction of valuable information from raw data. It enabling people and establishments to make healthier decisions from warehoused, expended and managed data. Data science makes use of hypothetical, accurate, computational and various other means to evaluate data. The main purpose is to extract the precise information that can be utilized for multiple reasons, including decision making, development of products, understand the trend and predicting.

Data science is a new, exponentially growing field, consisting of a set of tools and techniques used to extract valuable information from data. Data science is an interdisciplinary, problem-solving oriented subject that teaches to apply scientific techniques to practical problems. It focuses on practical classes and SME guided self-study during preparation of datasets, visualizing the data and programming of data analysis tasks.

Subjects

Introduction to data science

This subject gives an overview on basic concepts of data science, techniques and tools, data collection and integration, exploratory data analysis, predictive modelling, descriptive modelling, data product creation, evaluation, and effective communication.

Data mining and predictive analytics

This subject provides an overview on hidden patterns of data through machine learning and sophisticated algorithms. This also make us to understand how to extract hidden value from newly discovered patterns using business knowledge.

Descriptive analytics

This subject describes about the raw data and make it something that is interpretable by humans. This subject also helps us to learn from past behaviors/event that had occurred and understand how they might influence future outcomes.
