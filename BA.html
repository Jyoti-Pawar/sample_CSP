Business Analytics

Business analytics (BA) is the techniques and methods used by an establishment to measure performance. It is the application of statistical methods to a product, process or project and used to evaluate an entire company. It identifies weaknesses in existing processes and highlight useful data that helps an organization to prepare for forthcoming challenges. The need for good business analytics has urged the conception of business analytics software and enterprise platforms that help in mining the data of the establishment to automate the processes and bring meaningful insights.
